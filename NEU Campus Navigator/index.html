Campus and Room Wayfinding

Get to your destination with ease using our integrated building and room wayfinding system.

With our user-friendly interface, navigating the New Era University Main Campus has never been easier. The system provides real-time directions, so you can easily find your way around campus. You can also search for specific buildings or rooms, view floor plans, and get information about campus facilities.

Emergency Evacuation Plan

Stay safe and secure during emergency situations with our clear and concise evacuation instructions.

In case of an emergency, our evacuation instructions will help ensure safety and well-being of all campus community members. Our system provides clear and concise instructions to follow during crisis situations, such as fires, earthquakes, or other emergencies.

Schedule Manager

Manage your schedule effortlessly with our user-friendly schedule management system.

Our user-friendly schedule management system allows users to view and manage their room assignments and timetables with ease. You can access your schedule from anywhere, and make changes as needed.
